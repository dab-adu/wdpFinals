PRODUCT CARE INSTRUCTIONS

Our flowers are fresh and in its current state upon delivery will last up to 3 - 5 days.With a little bit of tender loving care, they can last up to one week!

Take a look at these care tips so that you can keep your arrangement fresh and beautiful.

  • Our fresh bouquets come with water wrapped underneath to keep them hydrated while being delivered. You may choose to keep as it is or transfer to a vase.
  • Make sure you a clean vase - especially the interior, so that bacteria will not reduce the lifespan of your beautiful bouquet.
  • Take out wilting petals and leaves immediately to avoid contamination.
  • Remove any leaves below the water line. This will not only make your arrangement look better but also prevent bacterial growth.
  • Cut the stems diagonally with a knife and submerge the stems in the water.
  • Flowers stay longer when they're in the cold, keep them away from direct sunlight or any appliances that produce heat.
  • Keep your flowers away from window sills, vents and fans as this wil dehydrate your flowers.
  • Do not display your flowers near fruit! The ethylene gas emitted by the fruits can reduce the longevity of your fresh arrangement.
  • It’s also important to clean your vase thoroughly and change out the water every two to three days.
